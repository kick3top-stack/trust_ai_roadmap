# TrustAI Roadmap

## Vision

TrustAI aims to become a trust and verification layer for AI systems by providing verifiable execution receipts, transparent billing records, and audit-friendly generation provenance.

---

## Phase 1 - MVP (Completed)

### Goals

* Demonstrate cryptographic AI execution receipts
* Provide transparent credit accounting
* Support receipt verification
* Enable billing dispute workflows

### Features

* User authentication
* AI Playground
* Streaming generation
* Receipt generation
* Ed25519 signatures
* Merkle batching
* Receipt verification
* Billing ledger
* Support console
* Admin dashboard

Status:  Completed

## Long-Term Vision

TrustAI seeks to become a universal verification layer for AI services, allowing users, platforms, and enterprises to independently verify:

* What model executed
* What parameters were used
* What billing occurred
* Whether records were modified
* Whether outputs belong to a verified execution

The objective is not to replace AI providers, but to increase trust, transparency, and auditability across the AI ecosystem.
